Sunday, January 08, 2006

7th January 2005

I did a night run from Karen's flat in Walthamstow, intending to run to Leyton and back. However, being unfamiliar with the area meant that I got off track almost immediately and wandered off into parts unknown. I started at 7pm and ran for 75 minutes, which was good, but couldn't really figure out where I'd been from looking at the map afterwards. I started on Valentin Road, then turned right onto Wood Street, then onto Whipps Cross Road, down to the Green Man intersection. From there it's a bit fuzzy. I went through a housing development then came out the other side somewhere along the A12. Ended up going north along Hollybush Hill Road, turned at Snaresbrook tube station, down Wanstead High Street then somehow back to Leytonstone tube station and from there back to Green Man, then Whipps Cross Road, Wood Street, etc. There must have been an awful lot of wiggling about on the route because I covered a really small area of ground for the time I was out. Admittedly it was the World's Slowest Run, but even so...
