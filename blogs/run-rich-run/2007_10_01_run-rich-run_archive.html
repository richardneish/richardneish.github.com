Friday, October 05, 2007

Regents park loop - 8.7km

From home, via Abercorn Place to Abbey Road, down to St John's Wood Road, along to Park Road, then in to Regent's Park and once around the Outer Circle anti-clockwise, then home by the same route as on the way out.

I tried to make this a fast run, and am reasonably happy with my pace. Slightly slow at around 6-7km but overall felt faster than my normal training pace. Good time and distance for a light training run.

Terrain: flat on road
Distance: 8.7km
Time: 42:35 (TBC)
Pace: 4:53 min/km

http://www.gmap-pedometer.com/?r=1368689 (Abbey Gardens to Regent's Park)
http://www.gmap-pedometer.com/?r=1368692 (Regent's Park Outer Circle, anti-clockwise)

Monday, October 01, 2007

Robin Hood half-marathon 1:50:04

Very happy with Robin Hood half-Marathon results on 16/09/2007. Started fast, with Gordon, but he sped off and I settled down to solid 8:20-8:30 minutes per mile (approx 5:11-5:17 minutes per km), fading a bit towards the end. I really want to put in the long run training to be able to sustain this kind of aggressive pace over a marathon distance.

I took one 3-dose energy gel with me and fed at 3, 6 and 9 miles. Basically I took a mouthful of energy gel 100m before the water stop, then washed it down with two to three mouthfuls of water. Worked well, although I felt slightly nauseous running and eating.

Terrain: undulating
Distance: 21.1km
Time: 1:50:04
Pace: 5:13 min/km
