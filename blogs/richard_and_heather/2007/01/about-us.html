About Us


In case you don't know us both, here's a tiny shapshot of who we are and how we met.

Richard is Jamaican, and has been living in London since July 2002. He's an enthusiastic sailor, not-so-enthusiastic long distance runner and an avid technophile. He works in the City as a technical consultant providing computer software programming services to large large investment banks.

Heather is South African and has been living at various places in London and South-East England since January 2002. She likes the outdoors, reading and baking. She is a Speech and Language Therapist working for the NHS.


We met 1st July 2006 on a sailing trip in Greece. After seven days of adventure including major storms on the first day of the trip we definitely were interested in each other and started dating when we got back to London.


Much more to tell, but I'll stick this online now and come back to it later perhaps.
