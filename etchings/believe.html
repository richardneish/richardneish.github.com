Believe

Believe in the work you do.
Believe that it is important, that what you do makes life better for someone, somewhere.
Believe that you are good at what you do.
If you don't have these strongly held beliefs about the nature of yourself and your work, then you are doing the wrong thing. You will be a happier person doing something else. It also helps if you have evidence to back up these beliefs, but truth is overrated.

Richard Neish, 07 November 2003
