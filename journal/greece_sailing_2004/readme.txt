GREECE SAILING 2004
-------------------
To view the logs, photos and maps on this CD, open the file "index.html"
in your favourite web browser.  A frames-enabled browser is required 
(most browsers will be fine).

Alternately, you should be able to browse the maps and photos on a 
DVD player and TV if the DVD player can display JPEG images - read your 
manual if you are not sure.

Enjoy
